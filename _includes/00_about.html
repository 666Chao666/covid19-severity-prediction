In recent weeks, the novel coronavirus causing COVID-19 has dramatically changed the shape of our global society and economy, impacting the lives of almost every individual on the planet to an extent modern civilization has never experienced. Our group is working to help guide the allocation of medical supplies by (1) curating county-level and hospital-level data on covid-19 (including demographics, risk factors, social distancing data) and (2) build simple models for forecasting deaths at the county-level and predicting severity at the hospital-level.
